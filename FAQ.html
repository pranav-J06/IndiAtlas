General Information

1. How many states and union territories does India have?
India currently has 28 states and 8 union territories.

2. What is the capital of India?
New Delhi is the capital of India.

3. When did India gain independence?
India gained independence from British rule on 15 August 1947.

4. When did India become a republic?
India became a republic on 26 January 1950, which is celebrated as Republic Day.

5. What is the national language of India?
India does not have a single national language. Hindi and English are used as the primary official languages for the central government.

Geography & Environment

6. How many national parks are there in India?
India has over 100 protected national parks serving as safe havens for diverse wildlife.

7. How many UNESCO World Heritage Sites are there in India?
India boasts more than 40 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, highlighting its historical depth and environmental beauty.

8. Which is the largest state in India by area?
Rajasthan is the largest state by land area.

9. Which is the smallest state in India by area?
Goa is the smallest state by land area.

10. Which river is the longest in India?
The Ganga (Ganges) is the longest river flowing entirely within India.

Culture & Society

11. How many languages are spoken in India?
India is incredibly linguistically diverse, with over 120 major languages and hundreds of regional dialects spoken across the country.

12. What is the national animal of India?
The magnificent Bengal Tiger is the national animal.

13. What is the national bird of India?
The beautiful Indian Peacock is the national bird.

14. What currency is used in India?
The official currency is the Indian Rupee (INR).
